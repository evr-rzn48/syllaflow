### Syllable-Direction Algorithm
Unlike traditional word searches where words follow straight lines, SyllaFlow changes direction at natural syllable boundaries, creating:
- **Meditative pause points** that mirror breath patterns
- **Linguistic awareness** of word structure and rhythm  
- **Contemplative gameplay** that transforms puzzle-solving into moving meditation
- **Cultural connection** through etymology and word origins

### Mindfulness Integration
Every aspect of SyllaFlow is designed to support contemplative practice:
- **Breathing synchronization** with word discovery rhythm

- **Community features** fostering connection and mutual support
